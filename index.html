Chapter Four

The breakthroughs.

What was broken. What I built. How it changed the business.

01

Digital Scan

Behavioral AI Engine

Problem

Teams needed fast, accurate behavioral insight but were stuck with long questionnaires and manual research.

Solution

Built an AI engine that generates science-backed behavioral insight from just a name, job title, and company. No surveys. No forms.

Impact

Insight in seconds instead of hours. Used across hiring, sales, and client relationships.

02

Discovery SDK

Embeddable Behavioral Module

Problem

Companies wanted behavioral intelligence inside their apps but couldn’t justify a full rebuild.

Solution

Designed a drop-in SDK that delivers the full discovery experience with customizable depth, multi-language support, and webhook tracking.

Impact

Faster integrations. Wider adoption. Scales across platforms with minimal effort.

03

Gene Facilitate

AI Behavioral Conversation Engine

Problem

Users struggled to understand behavioral insights without a human guiding them.

Solution

Built an AI assistant that interprets behavioral data, explains insights in plain language, and generates advisor-ready summaries instantly.

Impact

Clearer decisions. Faster onboarding. Zero manual interpretation needed.

04

Watchdog System

120+ Endpoint Monitoring

Problem

Critical systems failed silently and teams only found out from users.

Solution

Built an automated monitoring and self-healing layer for 120+ endpoints, including alerting, auto-recovery, and escalation rules.

Impact

Near-zero downtime. Issues fixed before teams even hear about them.

05

Operational Automation Suite

90% Workflow Automation

Problem

Manual workflows created errors, delays, and cross-team bottlenecks.

Solution

Automated core operational processes across engineering, marketing, and leadership using AI and system design.

Impact

90%+ workflows automated. 40+ hours saved weekly. ~85% error reduction.

06

Financial Behavior & Work Talent Reports

Insight Clarity Redesign

Problem

Core behavioral reports were dense, overwhelming, and failed to highlight the insights that mattered for real decisions.

Solution

Redesigned the Financial Behavior and Work Talent reports with clearer scoring logic, improved insight narratives, modern visuals, and stronger behavioral modeling.

Impact

Sharper insights. Faster understanding. Better advisor and client conversations.
